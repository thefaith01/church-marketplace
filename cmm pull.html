WHAT THIS APP IS =========================================================== A private, church-verified marketplace where Christians hire each other for services. Trust comes from church verification, not public ratings. Three roles: - MEMBER: can browse listings, message providers, create booking requests - PROVIDER: can create/manage listings, receive messages and bookings - ADMIN: can verify users, manage churches/listings/bookings The verification gate is the central business rule: browsing, messaging, and bookings are ALL LOCKED until an admin sets a user's verificationStatus to VERIFIED.
